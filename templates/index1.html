INSTRUCTIONS


• Sit in a well lit area.

• Ensure that permission for web cam is granted.

• Make sure that you are not interrupted by background movement during the translation.

• Ensure that you have a stable internet connection.

• Ensure that your hand is positioned within the square frame.

• Contrasting backgrounds are preferred.

• Press "." to close the webcam.

• Take help from instructional video as and when necessary.
